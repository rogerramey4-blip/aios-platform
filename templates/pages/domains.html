How it works
1 Register your domain above — we generate your DNS records

3 Add the TXT verification record to prove domain ownership
4 Click "Verify DNS" — your dashboard is live at your custom domain with full HTTPS/SSL
Security guarantees

  • All data encrypted at rest with AES-256
